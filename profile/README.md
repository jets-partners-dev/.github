## What we do

We build software for clients who need things to work. Web apps, backend systems, cloud infrastructure, and IoT integrations. Most of our work is for aviation and travel companies, but we take on projects in other industries when the fit is right.

Based in Morocco, working with clients in Europe and the US.

## Projects

| Project | What it is | Status |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| [Jets & Partners](https://jets.partners) | Website for a private aviation advisory firm (charter, medevac, aircraft sales). Based in Casablanca. | Live |
